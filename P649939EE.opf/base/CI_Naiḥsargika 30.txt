30. These are the proper medicines which have been declared by the Blessed One to be partaken
of by sick monks: clarified butter, oil, honey, and molasses. These, having been held by
him for seven days at the most, to be used as a stored supply, should be consumed by the
sick monk if he wishes. Should he eat in excess of that, that is a niḥsargika-pāyāntika.
