28. When a month of the hot season remains, a varsasatika robe may be searched about for by
a monk. When a half-month remains, having made it up, it should be put on. If a monk should
search about for a varsasatikii robe with less than a month of the hot season remaining,
or should put It on, having made it after a half-month .of the hot season remains, that is
a niḥsargika-pāyāntika.
