59. Whatever monk should pick up with his own hand or cause a jewel or what is considered to
be ajewel to be picked up, except in the ararna or in the āvāsa, that is a pāyāntika, A jewel
or what is considered to be a jewel should be picked up by a monk in the arama or in
the āvāsa, thinking, “This will be for he who will take it.” This is the proper course in
this matter.
