and ill-tempered. should snatch away or cause [the robe] to be snatched away, and should
say to him, in taking it back: “Moreover, O monk, I did not give the robe to you”; that robe
and that remainder"< should be forfeited by that [angry] monk to the one who made use
of it and that is a nil)sargika-pāyāntika.
