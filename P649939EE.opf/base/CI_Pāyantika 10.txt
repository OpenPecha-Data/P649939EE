10. Whatever monk, when the half-monthly Prātimokṣa Sūtra recitation is being recited, should
say: “What is the use, O Venerable Ones, of reciting these lesser and minor precepts in
the half-monthly Prātimokṣa Sūtra recitation, as they are conducive to remorse. sorrow, perplexity,
regret, and contrition for the monks”; in the transgression of the moral precepts,
there is a pāyāntika.
